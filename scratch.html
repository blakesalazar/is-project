My Favorite Moments From My First Year at BYU

Visiting National Parks

I took several trips to National Parks throughout the year. I went to Bryce Cannyon, Arches and Yellowstone. In each of these places I took went on several hikes and was able to explore nature to an extent that I never had before. It sparked an interest in nature that has created a bucket list of more national parks that I want to enjoy in my life.

Skiing

Some of my best memories from my freshman year include the several times I went skiing. I did not buy a season pass and instead went to several different resorts. This allowed my to go to several resorts with different friends. My favorite resorts are listed in the following order.

  1. Brighton Ski Resort
  2. Beaver Mountain Ski Resort
  3. Brian Head Ski Resort

Hanging out with friends

Lastly, I will never forget all the memories that came with hanging out with my friends. Living at the dorms allowed me to meet several new people and spend countless hours with them. Some of the things that I did with them are as follows.

  • Staying up watching tv
  • Pulling several all nighters
  • Hiking all around the area
  • Road trips to St. George
